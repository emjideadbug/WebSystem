      Frequently Asked Questions

      (FAQs)

      What is this website about?

      This is a cryptocurrency exchange simulation platform designed for users to experience trading without real financial risk. It provides real-time price charts, buy/sell order forms, an order book, trade history, and market depth analysis to mimic real-world trading platforms like Binance and Coinbase.

      How does the AI Investment Advisor work?

      Our AI Investment Advisor is a floating chatbot that provides general investment strategies for cryptocurrency, stocks, and mobile banking. It uses real-time Bitcoin market rates from Coinlib.io and has predictive FAQs to help users make informed decisions.

      Can I trade real cryptocurrency on this platform?

      No, this is a simulation meant for learning and practice. It does not involve actual financial transactions but mirrors real market data for an authentic trading experience.

      Is the website mobile-friendly?

      Yes! The website is fully responsive, optimized for both mobile and desktop users with a dark mode theme for an enhanced user experience.

      What cryptocurrencies are supported in the simulation?

      The platform primarily tracks Bitcoin and other major cryptocurrencies, providing real-time price updates and trading analytics.

      How is this different from real exchanges like Binance or Coinbase?

      Unlike real exchanges, this platform is a simulated environment with no real financial risks. However, it is designed to closely resemble real-world exchanges in terms of interface, trading tools, and analytics.
